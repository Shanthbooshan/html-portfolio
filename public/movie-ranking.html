The Best Movies According to Shanth Oreo

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Interstellar

"Interstellar" is a visually stunning sci-fi epic that masterfully explores the depths of space and time, captivating audiences with its emotional depth and mind-bending narrative.

Oppenheimer

"Oppenheimer" is a thought-provoking documentary that delves into the complex persona of J. Robert Oppenheimer, shedding light on his pivotal role in the development of the atomic bomb and the moral dilemmas he faced.

Pursuit of Happiness

"Pursuit of Happiness" is a poignant and inspirational film that showcases Will Smith's compelling performance, offering a heartfelt portrayal of resilience and the pursuit of one's dreams against all odds.
